The Manifesto
We're done making billionaires.

For too long, the deal has been simple and unfair: you bring the talent, you do the work, and someone at the top takes the fortune. You trade your freedom for a paycheck — and the constant fear that it could vanish tomorrow.

Forged Pixel is built on one belief: the people who make things should be the ones who prosper from them. Not shareholders. Not absentee owners. Not a CEO making four hundred times what the creators do. You.

You choose your projects. You choose your team. You decide how the rewards are shared. We don't own your work — and we can't fire you from your own dream.

Getting wealthy is not the problem. How you get wealthy is. Money earned from your own work and talent — shared fairly with the people who built it beside you — is good and right. What we reject is wealth squeezed out of the labor of people kept powerless below you.

So we built this differently, on purpose. Forged Pixel will never be sold off, never go public, never be hollowed out. It's locked to its mission — permanently. And as we succeed, our profits flow back out: into communities from Nairobi to Manila to the small town nobody's investing in, so the next generation of creators gets the same shot you did.
